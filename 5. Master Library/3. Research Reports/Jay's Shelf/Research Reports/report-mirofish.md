## What It Is
MiroFish is a swarm intelligence simulation engine that deploys thousands of AI agents with distinct personalities to model and forecast complex real-world scenarios.

## What It Does
Users upload seed material (reports, documents, stories) and describe what they want to predict. MiroFish constructs a "high-fidelity parallel digital world" from that information and runs thousands of agents through it to generate scenario forecasts and what-if analyses. Demonstrated use cases include public opinion prediction and literary/narrative outcome forecasting. A web interface allows users to interact with simulated agents after the simulation completes. Built with Python (58%) and Vue (41%), licensed under AGPL-3.0. Latest release v0.1.2, March 7, 2026 — **no updates since launch**; still at v0.1.2 as of June 2026, last commit 2026-03-07. Stars have grown to 65,368 (up from 44.8k), with only 2 contributors. Active web app at mirofish.ai remains live.

## Relevance to This Project
The initial project note asks whether MiroFish could work as a "buddy for Pete" (the Python Specialist). The honest assessment: not really in the current phase. MiroFish is a scenario simulation tool, not a coding or trading assistant. Its swarm-intelligence approach is conceptually interesting for future use cases — particularly if Jay ever wants to stress-test trading strategies by simulating market participant behavior. That is a genuinely compelling future application, but it is firmly Phase N+many, not now. Worth parking on the shelf for that future scenario work.
