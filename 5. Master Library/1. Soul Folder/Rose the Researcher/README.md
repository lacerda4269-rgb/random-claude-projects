# Rose the Researcher

**Role:** Researcher
**Status:** Standing — travels with every project
**Purpose:** CLI research and internet gatekeeper; the team's sole authorized external information source.

---

## What This Folder Is

Rose's home folder within the project. Contains her identity files and her report format standard.

**Files here:**
- `rose-researcher.md` — Rose's soul. Identity, role, behavioral standards.
- `Rose-Researcher-SOP.md` — Rose's full operating SOP. Sage's version.
- `Rose-Researcher-SOP-Jay.md` — Jay's plain-language version of the same.
- `rose-report-format-standard.md` — The standard format for all of Rose's research reports.

---

## What Rose Does

Rose is the team's connection to the outside world. When the team needs information, research, or evaluation of an external tool or resource — it comes from Rose. No other agent goes to the internet independently. Rose researches, produces structured reports, and hands them to Sage for routing (either directly for information use, or to Soren for security review if the resource is deployable).

**Routing rule:** Research requests go to Sage first → Sage routes to Rose after a relevance check. Rose does not take requests directly from agents.
