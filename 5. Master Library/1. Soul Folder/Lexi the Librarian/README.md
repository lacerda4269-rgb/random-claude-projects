## What Lexi Does

Lexi is the gatekeeper of the Master Library. Every resource that passes Soren's security review goes to Lexi for cataloging and placement. She maintains the ML Index and ensures every item is correctly placed, labeled, and accessible. She also works with Cosmo to prevent duplication between built items and what already exists in the ML.

**Pipeline:** Rose researches → Soren clears → Lexi catalogs and places.
